## Physics Model Specifications

### Core Model: Kawastony Alternative Lambda-CDM

**Description:**
A novel quantum gravity-based approach to cosmology that potentially replaces the standard Lambda-CDM model with improved predictions for dark matter, dark energy, and large-scale structure formation.

**Key Features:**
- Derived from first principles quantum gravity
- Eliminates need for exotic dark energy/matter
- Consistent with observed universe expansion
- Testable predictions for next-generation telescopes

**Mathematical Foundation:**

## Key Equations & Concepts

### Alternative Cosmological Model
```
Metric: ds² = -c²dt² + a(t)² dΩ²
```

Where the scale factor `a(t)` is derived from quantum gravity principles rather than empirical Lambda-CDM assumptions.

**Core Prediction:**
- Universe expansion consistent with observations
- No need for dark energy equation of state parameter (w = -1)
- Quantum effects modify large-scale geometry
